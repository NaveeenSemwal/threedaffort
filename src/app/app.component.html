1.a. Understanding the Project requirement The team gets the complete briefing with the client. This information is collated and assigned to different departments, Inputs are then sorted, filtered & presented in a clear & concise manner to the client.

1.b. Submission for review
A review is held between the client & our team to ensure that all are on the same page with respect to the project demands.

1.c. A Standard form of quotation is signed after reviewing all the aspects of projects which includes time, extra costs & any other miscellaneous expenditure. This quotation is forwarded to the client.

1.d. Final Agreement
Once the final budget is approved by the client, the concerned department initiate the documentation of final agreement with the client.
Before drafting the final agreement, the document cleared 'under 1.b.' is signed off to set up the project scope in totality.

Subsequently, the functions of design project management planning framework generally include the following:
1. Scope Management : Indicators describe the projectâ€™s overall objectives, purpose, and results in operational and measurable terms. They enable reliable monitoring and evaluation of the project. Specification of project objectives and plans including delineation of scope, budgeting, scheduling, setting performance requirements.

2. Schedule Management: Implementation of various operations through proper coordination and control of planning, design, estimating, contracting in the entire process with in a stipulated time period.

3. Quality Management : Maximization of efficient resource utilization through available resources by adopting latest technologies & software along with the expert & skillful design professionals

4. HR management : Assign a dedicated project team i.e. An Architect,3D designers, quality testing engineer, Other relevant professionals based on the project requirements & an exclusive 'Project Manager'.

3.a Design Development Based on the mutually agreed scope document & design development documents consisting of sketches, drawings and other documents to fix and describe the size and character of the entire design project in the form of 'Clay-Model' will be developed and presented accordingly.Once a 'Clay-Model' is developed, it is presented to the client for review and adjustment to the design. The design is then finalized upon approval by the client.

3.b Drawing Development Once the Design Development Documents are approved, the assigned team then move on to the architectural drawings and specifications phase. These are accurate and scaled drawings that set forth in detail the requirements of the approved design. These include all floor plan, exterior elevations, interior elevations, cross sections, site plan, and architectural details with material specification ( subject to the availability). Basically all drawings and specifications that related to the aesthetics of the project subject to the project scope.

The assigned Project manager ensure the competencies & availability of human resources to provide the service required and advise the client accordingly within a stipulated time frame. Clients can directly rely on him/her & ask about the project progress.

A project manager takes the final approval & clearance of successfully completion of project & ask client for any further alteration if required. After taking the final satisfactory call from the client, A final invoice is sent to the client along with the feedback form.
